References

The Effect of Income and Wealth on Time and Money Spent on Food" Author: Jennifer M. Mellor Published in: American Journal of Agricultural Economics, 2008. This study examines how income and wealth impact both the amount of money spent on food and the time allocated to food-related activities within households. "Food Expenditure Patterns in the United States: The Role of Food Away From Home" Authors: Biing-Hwan Lin and Jonq-Ying Lee Published in: USDA Economic Research Report, 2011. This report from the U.S. Department of Agriculture analyzes food expenditure patterns, considering the influence of household income and the shift toward food away from home. "Income, Food Expenditure, and Health: Evidence from China" Authors: Linxiu Zhang, Jikun Huang, and Scott Rozelle Published in: The Review of Economics and Statistics, 2010. This study investigates the relationship between income, food expenditure, and health outcomes in rural China, providing insights into the impact of income on food choices and nutrition. "Household Income, Food Expenditure, and Childhood Food Insecurity in South Korea" Authors: Seung-Sup Kim and Shudong Zhang Published in: International Journal of Environmental Research and Public Health, 2019. This research explores the relationship between household income, food expenditure, and childhood food insecurity in the context of South Korea.
